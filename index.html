About me

I'm a Data Science student at UC San Diego passionate about turning raw data into decisions that matter. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, data engineering, and real-world impact, from modeling consumer credit risk for fintech partners to building full-stack analytics systems that drive business growth.

I bring both technical depth and real-world context to every project. I've engineered ML pipelines over millions of transactions, built production-ready web applications, and led interdisciplinary teams, all while working part-time and maintaining academic honors.
